Atmospheric Environment Decision Intelligence

A locally deployed analysis platform integrating AI agents, multimodal data acquisition, model analysis, and causal inference.

AirMosaic AI provides standardized tools — data catalog, acquisition pipelines, model analysis methods, and causal inference frameworks — for external AI agents and local research scripts. No data is bundled in the repository. Everything is fetched on demand.
